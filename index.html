    Our Story

    The goal is to cater Pan Asian, Continental, Arabic, Indian, Chinese cuisine under one roof. Alongside, Our menu is curated with a Desi Swag Progressive twist, lip smacking food with contemporary platting style one in a kind.

    About the Company

    We, at The Old School Café, are firm believers that dining isn’t just about eating and exiting. It is about the dining experience as a whole.

    Antiquis is a Latin word that means “Old”. For our generation, “old school” are the 70’s and the 80’s - the golden era of Rock and Pop. The magic of that era translates into our ambiance, décor, music, and food. And trust us when we say that we have infused the goodness of the Indian retro in our food!

    At “The Old School Café”, we serve Pan-Asian and Continental Fusion food. We have found the perfect way to blend Western food with Indian flavours. Each dish that we serve, has a star Indian ingredient or spice which unmistakeably shines through. Our spices and masalas, along with the recipes are unique to us as is the gastronomic experience you are assured to have.

    Ambiance and Décor

    We are not open for dine-ins as the safety of our customers is of primary importance to us. When we open, get ready to go on a nostalgic trip down your memory lane. When we say that this will bring back memories from your past, we kid you not. Does Snakes and Ladders, radio, cassettes, video player, Popeye, Tom and Jerry, Michael Jackson, Backstreet Boys, Led Zeppelin etc. remind you of your childhood/teenage years? This is exactly what you will find imbibed in our décor. The decor is synchronized with the music we play. It is indeed our motive to transport you back to those times and satisfy your taste buds with some mouth- watering food.
