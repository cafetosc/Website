Azharuddin Siddiqui

Founder

"The vision teller tells the vision to unguarded minds' of prey. The programmed." ― T.F. Hodge (From Within I Rise: Spiritual Triumph Over Death and Conscious Encounters with “The Divine Presence ”)

With more than 7 years of corporate experience Azhar has imbibed the essence of people management and interpersonal skills. Having a dream in mind and strong will to execute it, he has stood against the odds of nature to start this venture at the time when the world has taken the worst economic hit this generation has ever seen.

He is full of life and positive energy that transfers to the people and the space around. With a good sense of humour, he lifts the spirits of his colleagues and everybody he is surrounded with. He loves playing football, cricket, snooker, chess and carrom . He has immense love for music and dance and respect for artists. Azhar is a family -oriented person and likes to spend his free time with his family.

Azhar has put together a like-minded team where everyone in the team has a unique skill set and together, they are willing to go beyond their limits to make their customers happy. He and his team have aligned to a goal of serving “good quality and tasty food at an affordable price".

Vikas PD

Co-Founder

Drone pilot, bike racer, an out-and-out techie are just a few adjectives that describe Vikas. A techie from the Aerospace Industry, Vikas is indeed a jack of all trades. He is the go-to-guy for any technical obstacle we come across. Interestingly, it is his passion for bikes and riding that swung him in the direction of opening a restaurant.

Riders have a Sunday ritual(I am sure a lot of you will relate to) – get up early in the morning, catch up with fellow riders, go on a long refreshing ride and spend some time in a café, with good food and good conversations flowing. As the cafes they frequented got all too familiar, Vikas felt the options where he and his biker buddies could hang out and have some amazing food, were limited. This inspired him to collaborate with long-time friend, Azharuddin Siddiqui. Vikas and Azhar are college buddies and have known each other for more than 10 years. While their careers took them separate ways, their friendship and mutual respect has only grown stronger with time. When Azhar shared his plans to open up a restaurant, it was an easy decision for Vikas to make.


Ahalya Prabhakaran

Co-Founder

A childhood spent in her father’s restaurant and love for food, is what inspired Ahalya to step into the food industry. “Dining is not about eating and exiting. It is about what pleases your taste buds, tummy and ultimately your soul.” – this is what Ahalya believes in. The most cherished memories that Ahalya has is somehow related to food. It is either the people with whom she was with while dining or the food itself, or both.This is what inspired her to embark on this culinary endeavour. A home baker and a complete sucker for traditional baking recipes, especially pies and pastries, she wants to give all the sweet tooth lovers something to absolutely die for!

2020 has been a tough year for most. Butthis year has also given Ahalya what she considers the two greatest gifts. While the first one is a blessing on the personal front, the second one is both personal and professional - the opening of Antiquis – The Old School Café. Azharuddin Siddiqui, the founder, happens to be a very good friend of Vikas, through whom she knows Azhar. Years of knowing each other proved to be a boon in this transition from friends to business partners.

Syed Mutahir Ahamed

An MBA graduate from M.S RAMAIAH, Syedstarted his career in the E- commerce industry with Alibaba and is now a part of Amazon. His true passion has always been to pursue a career in the F&B sector.

Syed has anoverall experience of more than 4 years in Business development and Vendor Management. His expertise helps the company in CRM and Operations.
